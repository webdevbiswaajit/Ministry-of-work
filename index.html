Overview of the
Ministry of Works

Overview of the Ministry of Works is located on John F Kennedy Drive, New Providence, TheBahamas.

The Hon. Desmond Thomas Bannister, MP serves as Minister of Works.

The portfolio of matters for which the Ministry of Works:

  • Department of Ministry Works
  • Construction, maintenance and upkeep of public infrastructure including Government buildings, roads, roadsides, verges, parks, drains, docks, bridges, cemeteries, Montague beach foreshore, and Western esplanade.
  • Buildings Control Division
  • Explosives and volatile substances regulation
  • Construction standards and guidelines management.

Overview of the
Department of Ministry Works

The Department Public Works is headed by a Director who is a Chartered Engineer and Chartered Environmentalist along with two Deputy Directors. Its sections and their functions are outlined below and demonstrated in the following link to the Organisation Chart:

Architectural Section

The Architectural Section provides major professional services within the Design and Construction Division. It provides architectural services to numerous Government Ministries, Department Government Corporations.

Building Control

This section is responsible for the regulation of building construction in The Bahamas through the administration of The Building Regulations Act and The Building Regulations (General) Rules.
